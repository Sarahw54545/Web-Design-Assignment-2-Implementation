Why Waterford

SETU Facilities

Sports Clubs and Societies

SETU Waterford offers a wide range of clubs and societies for students to join.

There is a huge diversity of activities to choose from with over 80 sports clubs and societies catering for all standards, from national and international athletes to the individual who would like to try a new activity, join a group, or increase their social circle while enjoying their time at SETU.

SETU Sports Page

SETU Arena

The SETU Arena is the largest sports, conference, and events centre in the South East of Ireland.

This multipurpose facility caters for a wide range of activities, such as recreational gym users, high-performance sports athletes, multipurpose sports, camps, entertainment events, and conferences.

The facilities are state-of-the-art, and the service is truly exceptional.

SETU Arena Page

Student Accomodation

The College Campus consists of 66 Single En-suite rooms, of which 3 are suitable for disabled access. These apartments are located on the Main Campus, with easy access to all SETU services and facilities.

Facilities include:Wi-Fi, Resident Parking, Laundry Facility, Live-in Warden, CCTV System, 24 Hours Security

College Campus is located on the West side of the city, on the grounds of SETU's Main Campus in Waterford. It is on a direct bus route into the City Centre. Eircode: X91-CF21.

Student Union

The SETU student union provide students with a range of supports including career guidance, academic aids and emotional advice.

They also organise various events for the student body such as Freshers Week for students to meet new people and RAG week to raise money for charity.

They ensure that students' voices are heard.

Recreational Facilities

Waterford City offers modern leisure centers equipped with state-of-the-art gym facilities, swimming pools, and sports courts.

Outdoor enthusiasts can explore the scenic landscapes and walking trails in the city's parks and green spaces, perfect for picnics, jogging, or simply unwinding amidst nature.

Waterford's vibrant cultural scene is showcased through its theaters, art galleries, and museums, offering enriching experiences for those seeking artistic inspiration and cultural immersion.

Off Campus Accomodation

The SETU Student Pad is a free platform for students to find available accomodation around Waterford City. It was created in order to combat the recent student accomodation shortages, it has proven to be a great success.

Householders and Landlords have an excellent opportunity to market their rooms or properties to a ready made audience.

Student Supports

Counselling

The Counselling Service offers a student-centred, professional and confidential psychological support service to students who are in emotional or psychological distress about any issue or area of concern impacting their personal or academic well-being.

Services are available by appointment to full-time registered students and are free of charge.

Sports Scholarships

SETU sees the growing need for talented athletes to successfully juggle both their academic and sporting responsibilities and promotes their holistic development.

The Sports Scholarship programme at SETU helps realise this aim. With the help of a network of support services and experience, the Sports Scholarship programme is an athlete-led development strategy that is adapted to each athlete's unique requirements.

Career Development Center

The Careers Development Centre, provides professional careers service and is the main point of contact students, academic staff and employers.

The Careers Development Centre supports the University by providing opportunities for students and graduates to develop the skills and attributes required to manage their careers throughout their working lives. In person and online appointments are available.
