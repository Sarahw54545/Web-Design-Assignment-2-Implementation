Course Description

Welcome to the dynamic world of Exercise Sciences (Common Entry), a cutting-edge four-year degree program designed for those passionate about delving into the realm of exercise science while still exploring diverse specialisation avenues.

This innovative curriculum offers an immersive experience for students aspiring to carve out a niche in the realm of exercise science, providing a robust foundation for future success. Tailored for individuals keen on a career in Exercise Science but undecided on their precise focus or professional trajectory, this program offers a unique blend of academic rigor and hands-on exploration. Over the initial two years, students engage in a comprehensive exploration of Exercise Science, laying the groundwork for informed decision-making regarding their specialised path.

From Fitness and Movement to Nutrition and Positive Psychology, the curriculum encompasses a broad spectrum of subjects, augmented by practical placements to reinforce theoretical learning. As students progress, they have the opportunity to select from three distinct specialist exit pathways, culminating in a specialized BSc (Hons) degree tailored to their career aspirations. Join us on this transformative journey, where every step propels you closer to realizing your full potential in the dynamic field of Exercise Sciences.
